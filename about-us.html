About Us

We are a FIRST Robotics team from Calumet Michigan. We compete at local and state events every year, making a new robot and forming alliances with other teams from all over the state. We have a dedicated team of students and alumni who build (and help) make our robot each year to compete with other FRC teams. For an FRC competition, we build a robot to perform specific tasks and race against other teams to earn points for successfully completing challenges. As part of our team, we gain hands-on experience with CAD, 3D printing, rapid prototyping, Programming, and CNC machining. We also acquire teamwork skills such as organization, creativity, work ethic, communication, and collaboration. We spend a considerable amount of time working on our robot during build season, and by the end of the year, we form a tight-knit team.
